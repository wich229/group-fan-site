1. There are more than 10,000 hydrothermal features in Yellowstone. The four types of thermal features are geysers, hot springs, mud pots, and fumaroles.

Morning Glory Pool is a hot spring in the Yellowstone Upper Geyser Basin of the United States. The distinct color of the pool is due to bacteria which inhabit the water.

2. Yellowstone is home to the largest concentration of mammals in the lower 48 states (67 species).

Yellowstone is home to two species of bears: grizzly bears(above) and black bears. Of the two species, grizzly bears have a much smaller range across the United States.

3. There are approximately 1,000 miles of hiking trails in Yellowstone.

The Mystic Falls trailhead is a beginner’s trail at Yellowstone National Park. The trailhead is located at Biscuit Basin, a 10-minute drive from the Old Faithful Inn. After 2.5 miles (round trip), you’ll have seen a spectacular waterfall.
